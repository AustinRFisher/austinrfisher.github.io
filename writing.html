WRITING

EXPERIENCE

Over the past few years, I wrote high-end strategic plan documentation for the University of Waterloo, and took on the role of writer/editor in many industry-level projects.

I’ve written marketing material for videos and client presentations — but I really enjoy script-writing. In my spare time, I write novels, scripts, and screenplays. One TV spec script is completed — I can supply it upon request. Be nice to it, please.

This past year, I’ve expanded into tech blogging, writing articles on Medium about UX principles, case studies, digital marketing strategies, and my endless opinions about Snapchat.

One article reached 30,000 viewers, while others were picked up and reposted by external blogs.

I take complicated concepts and summarize them into a digestable, understandable form.

My digital marketing copy is developed user-first — it optimizes the strongest SEO terms to maximize site traffic and ROI.

Episodes include “UX of the Gym”, “The UX of Mail”, “How to be Happy”, and the “Consequences of Intimate Technology”. Listen below!

I created “UX in the World” through an app called Anchor, which makes audio sharing and podcasting incredibly easy and intuitive (I wrote about it, too).
