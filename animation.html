ANIMATION

EXPERIENCE

The moment I opened After Effects, I unmasked my obsession with animation. I’ve made a handful of gifs and one promotional video using flat design animation thus far. Animating satisfies my obsession with fine details. The bulk of my graphic design is making assets for animation.

I’m currently creating an animated resume I call a “trailer” — it’ll be advertising the best product I can offer: me!

My animation inspirations are “Thought Café” and “Kurzgesagt — In A Nutshell”. The biggest reason? I strive to create educational videos similar to what they do.

These gifs were designed for my article: “Here’s Why You Should Just Give Up!” which can be found on Medium. Read the article for better context behind the animations.

The animation below was created as an instructional video for an industry project called “Emerge”. We were tasked with innovating a way to make waiting rooms more tolerable.

The animation and all graphics were created in a couple days on a tight deadline. Normally I would storyboard a video like this out, but time was of the essence! You can read more about the project here.
